The Pocket Neighborhood

Reimagining community living with intentional design.

South Haven Cottages brings the beloved pocket neighborhood concept to Oregon's Willamette Valley. Our community of 11 charming 2-story cottages is designed to foster genuine connections while honoring your need for privacy.

Drawing inspiration from architect Ross Chapin's groundbreaking work, each cottage faces a shared commons—a welcoming green space where neighbors naturally gather.

Inspired Design

Ross Chapin's pocket neighborhoods have transformed how we think about community housing. By placing homes around shared green spaces, these neighborhoods naturally encourage neighborly interaction.

At South Haven, front porches face the commons, parking is kept to the periphery, and every design decision asks "does this build community?"

"A pocket neighborhood is a cohesive cluster of homes gathered around shared commons."
— The Pocket Neighborhood Philosophy
